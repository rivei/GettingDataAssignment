                + 1..30  .Unique identifier assigned within volunteers

        Name of six activities  
                1. WALKING
                2. WALKING_UPSTAIRS
                3. WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS
                4. SITTING
                5. STANDING
                6. LAYING
